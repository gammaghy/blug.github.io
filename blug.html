Welcome to the Batam Linux User Group!

The Batam Linux User Group (BLUG) is the Indonesian Linux Lovers' Community (KPLI) located in Batam City as a forum for learning and developing the GNU/Linux operating system and Open Source Software for the people of Batam City, as a means of gathering for fans and Linux users who are in Batam City, are expected to be able to accommodate their needs as well as a means to share stories about the problems encountered in using Linux and the development of Linux itself for Linux lovers who are in Batam City.
